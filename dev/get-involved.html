Volunteer

Overnight Program: Lead Host to be part of the team providing overnight supervision to ensure a safe, warm and welcoming place to sleep overnight (8:00pm to 8:00am)
Community Caf√©: Bring food and eat together or send a hot dish some evening in a week (5:15-6:30pm)
Support Activities:
  • Evening - 8-10pm. Arrange and supervise activities associated with setup for the overnight accomodation program; showers, and other related tasks.
  • Morning - 6:30-8:00am. Overnight Host supervises the take down of sleeping gear, the restoration of the sleeping accomodation room to its daytime configuration, and the washing up of dishes, sweeping of floors and cleaning. Breakfast Hosts provide a simple breakfast (mostly items to which hot water can be added).
  • Other - Participate in Work Groups needed to locate appropriate resources for mental and physical health care, daily food, housing, recovery programs, employment and funding.

Be an Advocate

Part of a team of two to partner with a homeless person to assist in obtaining state benefits to which they are entitled (general relief, food stamps, access to medical care, employment, recovery services, SSI, birth certificates, food, clothing).
